Do's and Don'ts of making the most of a music festival.

DO'S

  • Have a meet up spot. Before you and your friends even start raging designate a meeting spot just in case people go missing. Because most people lose cell phone service, this is imperative.
  • Bring a plastic bag/zip lock bag. You do not know how many times I have gotten to festival and on the first day my phone gets water logged and is destroyed. Phone=communication. YOU NEED IT. Put your phone, black berry, pager, whatever you have, in a plastic bag so that it doesn’t get wet. You will thank me later.
  • Have your phone fully charged! I cannot emphasize this enough. Once you loose your friends in a mob of ravers, and have no phone good look finding them again.
  • Have a buddy system. Like when your 5th grade teacher told you on school fieldtrips to have a buddy system, she was right.
  • Hydration.Hydration.Hydration. You try dancing in 90 degree weather in Miami for three days straight without a sip of water. Bad Idea.

DON'Ts

  • Girls, Don't dress to kill. Leave your stilettos at home. Wear comfortable sneakers that you don’t mind ruining because, honey your sneakers will be destroyed by Day 2.
  • Don't forget your manners! You think I'm going to let you through this mosh pit with you yelling and pushing- I don't think so. O and don't forget to give people their personal space.

Knick Knacks and Tricks

  • If you’re a germaphobe like me, I suggest you bring hand sanitizer. There is a CVS and Walgreens literally right across the street from Bayfront. Just run in there quickly and get your goodies.
  • Wear a satchel or a fanny pack. Guys normally wear light back-backs and have never had a problem. Wear light clothes. Honestly, the less clothes the better.
  • Sunglasses are a must! As well as sunscreen!
  • If your a smoker, make sure you go into the festival with your cigarrete pack unopen, or else security will confiscate it. This goes for candies as well.
  • Most importantly. If something goes wrong, don't let it ruin your day! Enjoy. Smile. Rage on.
